ABOUT US!


TJ Designs is a jewellery brand founded in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia by Tala Aljifri.


TJ Designs & Our History

Our jewellery, inspired by y2k fashion represents the ultimate embodiment of confidence and style. Wearing TJ Designs jewellery attracts eyes everywhere.

Made with gold filled material and cubic zircona, each piece is unique and is designed to make you feel confident and powerful.

Tala Aljifri, the founder of TJ Designs was only 18 years old when she founded the brand. Inspired by modern day fashion trends, she began creating handmade jewellery inspired by her love for fashion.
