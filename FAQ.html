Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



SHOPPING AND ORDERING


Why didn't I recieve my full order?

If you have not recieved your full order, please contact us so we can help you.


Where can I purchase TJ Designs products?

The only place you can purchase real TMS SHOP products are on our TMS SHOP website. You cannot buy our products from any other place or site.


SHIPPING AND HANDLING


Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we have worldwide shipping.


How long will it take for my order to arrive?

National (Saudi Arabia) orders take from 5-7 days, international orders take from 12-25 days.


What if my order didn't arrive on time?

If your order is taking longer than expected, please contact us so we can identify the issue. Please note that near popular holidays, your order may take longer than the expected date to arrive.


My order got lost during shipping, what do I do?

If your order has gotten lost during the shipping process, please contact us for a refund or reshipment of the order.


RETURNS AND EXCHANGES


How long do I have to return or exchange an item(s)?

We allow returns within 7 days from the day you recieve your item(s), and 15 days for exchanges.


What if my item(s) is defective?

If there is any issues with your item(s) or defectiveness, please contact us 48 hours days from the day you recieve your product. Photo evidence must be sent.
